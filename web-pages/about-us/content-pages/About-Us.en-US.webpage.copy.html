Document Format Instructions:

Please ensure that any documents you upload follow a specific format for easy processing. This will help us extract and analyze the information accurately. Here's how your document should be formatted:

  • Section Headings: Each section of the document should have clear headings in bold, such as Section 1: Accident Details, Section 2: Initial Accident Complaints"", and so on.
  • Speaker Names and Dialogue: The text should include speaker names followed by their dialogue. For example:
    • Stuart Siegel
      • Can you tell me the date of your motor vehicle accident?
    • Mitch Adelstein
      • The date of the accident was April 25th, 2022.
  • Section End: You can use {END OF DOCUMENT} to indicate end of the entire document, respectively.
